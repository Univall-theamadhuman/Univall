Child Privacy Policy

Ensuring child safety, privacy, and compliance worldwide.

Introduction

This policy outlines how we handle children's personal information in compliance with COPPA and other international child data protection laws. We prioritize privacy, transparency, and security for young users.

Data Collection

  • No personal data is collected from children without prior verifiable parental consent.
  • Anonymous usage data (e.g., device type, app usage frequency) may be collected for performance improvements.

Parental Consent

We obtain explicit parental permission before collecting any personal data. Parents can request access to, modify, or delete their child’s data at any time through our Univall support team.

Third-Party Services

Third-party tools (like analytics) are limited to collecting non-identifiable data and must comply with relevant child privacy laws. We never sell or misuse children’s information.

Right to Deletion

Parents or guardians have full control over their child’s data and may request deletion by contacting our Univall team. We process all deletion requests within a reasonable timeframe.

Contact Us

For any questions regarding this policy or your child’s data, please reach out to us at univall@theunivall.com.
